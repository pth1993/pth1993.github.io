I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University (OSU), advised by Dr. Ping Zhang in the Artificial Intelligence in Medicine (AIMed) lab. Before coming to OSU, I received my bachelor's degree from FPT University, where I was supervised by Dr. Phuong Le-Hong. My research focuses on the intersection of machine learning (ML), trustworthy AI and their applications to healthcare and biomedicine.
